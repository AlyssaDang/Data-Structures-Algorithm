## Sorting Linear Data

** 1. Selection Sort: **

                  Space: O(1)
                  Time:
                        Worst: O(n^2)
                        Average: O(n^2)
                        Best: O(n^2)

**2. Insertion Sort: **

                  Space: O(1)
                  Time:
                        Worst: O(n^2)
                        Average: O(n^2)
                        Best: O(n)

**3. Heap Sort: **

                  Space: O(1)
                  Time:
                        Worst: O(nlog(n))
                        Average: O(nlog(n))
                        Best: O(nlog(n))
                                      
**4. Bubble Sort: **

                  Space: O(1)
                  Time:
                        Worst: O(n^2)
                        Average: O(n^2)
                        Best: O(n)
            
**7. Merge Sort: **

                  Space: O(n)
                  Time:
                        Worst: O(nlog(n))
                        Average: O(nlog(n))
                        Best: O(nlog(n))
            
**8. Quick Sort: **

                  Space: O(n)
                  Time:
                        Worst: O(n^2)
                        Average: O(nlog(n))
                        Best: O(nlog(n))
